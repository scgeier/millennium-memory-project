For this project, I interviewed 37 people, from all walks of life, and recorded more than five and a half hours of audio. The story took me from bus stations to farmers' markets to fishing piers. And a funny thing happened along the way. More often than not, in mid-conversation with a total stranger, I got the peculiar feeling that we already knew each other.

At first, I thought this was because we shared the same core set of memories from the turn of the millennium -- countdowns and computers and all that. But I soon realized it was something else, something more sublime.

This slidecast is my attempt to explain what that thing is, in three minutes.
